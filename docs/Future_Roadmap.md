# Future Roadmap

Version 1.0.0 delivers the core auction workflow and an advanced purely deterministic analytics engine. 

## V1.1: Quality of Life
- **PDF Report Export**: Allow users to download their Team's performance analytics report as a PDF.
- **Spectator Mode**: Allow users to join a room purely to observe the ledger and dashboard without taking up a franchise slot.

## V2.0: Deep Analytics
- **Live Leaderboard Animations**: Enhance the `/results/[roomId]` page with dynamic bar-chart races as the season projection is simulated.
- **Match-up Engine**: Simulate Head-to-Head games between two assembled Playing XIIs based on individual player stats vs specific bowling types.
